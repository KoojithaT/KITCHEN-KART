# Kitchen Kart - Future Vision

## Short-Term Goals
- **User Authentication:** Allow users to register, log in, and save their preferences.
- **Custom Recipe Upload:** Enable users to submit and store their own recipes with images.
- **Enhanced Filtering:** Introduce filters for cooking time, ingredients available, spice levels, and diet plans (e.g., keto, gluten-free).
- **Dark Mode:** Add theme switching to enhance usability at night.

## Medium-Term Goals
- **AI-Based Suggestions:** Recommend recipes based on user activity, preferences, and past saved recipes.
- **Ingredient-Based Recipe Generator:** Users can input ingredients they have, and the app will suggest recipes they can make.
- **Weekly Meal Planner:** A calendar-based feature for users to plan their meals in advance.
- **Shopping List Generator:** Based on chosen recipes, generate a shopping list automatically.

## Long-Term Vision
- **Voice Assistance Integration:** Add voice-controlled navigation for hands-free cooking (e.g., Alexa, Google Assistant).
- **Multilingual Support:** Add support for regional languages to cater to a broader user base.
- **Community Features:** Include recipe ratings, user comments, and a cooking forum.
- **Partnerships with Grocery Apps:** Link recipes to real-time grocery delivery platforms like BigBasket, Blinkit, etc.
